This website extends the ideas of Intimacy Without Proximity into a living, digital form. It gathers the references, sources, and materials that shaped the book, and presents them as an ongoing anthology built through links.

Designed with a minimal, text-focused approach influenced by early internet brutalism, the site foregrounds functionality and trace. Links appear with green underlines, shifting once visited to mark each user's path through the network. What you see here is less a collection of essays than a system of connections: abstracts, descriptions, and openings into larger bodies of work.

Visitors are invited to add their own sources, extending the network beyond the book and into shared space. Through these contributions, the site maps how knowledge moves between people who may never meet, yet remain in quiet conversation with one another.

Intimacy happens in the act of tracing how knowledge circulates between people who may never meet, but are still in conversation. This platform is a place to follow links, leave traces, and take part in the ongoing exchange of ideas.
